Our Story

Where Every Cake Tells a Story

Sweet Crumbs Bakery was born from a simple belief: every celebration deserves a cake as extraordinary as the moment itself. Founded by passionate baker Sofia Morales, we've been creating edible works of art in the heart of our community since 2014.

We use only the finest ingredients — real butter, farm-fresh eggs, premium Belgian chocolate, and organic flour — to ensure each cake is not only beautiful, but unforgettably delicious. Every design is crafted entirely by hand, with patience, creativity, and genuine care.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your custom cake order.

✿
How far in advance should I place my cake order?
+
We recommend placing your order at least 2–3 weeks in advance for custom cakes and 4–6 weeks for wedding or large event orders. Rush orders may be accommodated depending on availability — a rush fee may apply. During peak seasons (May–June and October–December) we fill up fast, so the earlier the better!
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?
+
Yes! We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and vegan options. Please mention your dietary needs clearly in the "Additional Notes" section of the order form, or call us to discuss. Note: while we take every precaution, our kitchen handles common allergens, so we cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment.
Do you offer cake delivery?
+
Yes! We offer local delivery within a 25-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees range from $15–$45 based on distance. For tiered cakes, we strongly recommend delivery over pickup to ensure safe transport. You can also choose free pickup from our shop during business hours.
Can I request a fully custom cake design?
+
Absolutely — custom designs are our specialty! Share inspiration photos, color swatches, or detailed descriptions. Once we receive your order, we'll schedule a brief consultation call to finalize every detail and ensure the cake perfectly captures your vision.
What is your payment and cancellation policy?
+
A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 7 days before the event. Cancellations more than 2 weeks out receive a credit toward a future order. Cancellations within 2 weeks are non-refundable due to ingredient preparation. We accept all major credit cards, Venmo, Zelle, and cash.
How should I store my cake once I receive it?
+
Most cakes should be refrigerated and taken out 30–60 minutes before serving for best flavor and texture. Fondant cakes are best kept at room temperature in a cool, dry spot to prevent condensation affecting the finish. All cakes come with personalized storage instructions. For best taste, enjoy within 3–5 days of delivery/pickup.
